03About Us

A father’s practice, a family’s calling.

Kasab Law was founded by the late Adv. Khaled Kasab, who opened his first practice in Nazareth and, case by case, earned a reputation that carried the firm to Jerusalem and Haifa. Today his son and daughter continue what he began — the same devotion to clients, renewed for a new generation.
